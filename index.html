Project Based Study

Project-Based Study (PBL) is a student-centered instructional strategy where learning occurs through the completion of projects. These projects are typically complex tasks or problems that require students to investigate, research, plan, collaborate, and present their findings or solutions. PBL integrates content knowledge with skills such as critical thinking, problem-solving, communication, and collaboration.
